On June 1, 2013, another year will be added to Vale’s history. Founded in 1942, in Itabira, Minas Gerais, today Vale is one of the largest mining companies in the world. Our main business is mining, an activity that is essential to modern life: from cell phones to airplanes, from the frames of buildings to coins, ores are important in the production of many objects that are indispensable to our everyday lives.

Dive into our history

Other options for those wishing to know everything about Vale include the book and the film, Our History. Produced in 2012, when Vale celebrated its 70th year of activities in Brazil, these are both important historical records.

The book was based on the publication produced in 1992 to commemorate the company’s 50th anniversary. The richly written 70 year anniversary book includes 20 years of history and a wide range of images. Download individual chapters or the complete version of the book in PDF.

The film, Our History, uses Vale employees as the main protagonists. Featuring real testimonies, the film is very moving and was winner of the Cannes Corporate Media & TV Awards, one of the most important corporate film festivals in the world held annually in France. The competition at the festival was considerable: the video went up against 666 other works from 35 different countries. The corporate films panel of judges is made up of past Oscar and Emmy winners, media and corporate image specialists, and marketing and communications specialists. Vale was the first non-European company to receive the award.
